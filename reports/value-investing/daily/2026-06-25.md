- **GIS — General Mills** → [deep-dives/GIS-2026-06-25.md](../deep-dives/GIS-2026-06-25.md)
  *Deep-value/"show-me": ~8.5x trailing / ~10.9x forward, fresh 52-wk low, ~36% off high, 7.1% dividend covered by ~$2.3B FCF. The catch is real — FY26 organic sales down and adjusted EPS guided **−16–20%**, and Q3 FY26 organic was −3%, so the volume problem must prove cyclical not secular. - **META — Meta Platforms** → [deep-dives/META-2026-06-25.md](../deep-dives/META-2026-06-25.md)
